Momentum Network.

Harnessing the
power of nature.

Individually we are all on the path to a green transition. The wind power industry is part of the foundation that will create a sustainable society in the future. That also leaves us with the responsibility to ensure that we provide sustainable value chains, and the most effective way to do that is to work together.

01.

Purpose

Momentum is a practical network of wind energy OEM suppliers. The future of OEMs will hold various demands from customers, governments, and the environment. The climate crisis has made it a necessity to drive down production costs to expand the adoption of clean wind energy globally and accelerate the transition to more sustainable means of production. We believe that global challenges must be met with a global mindset – a collaborative mindset of transparency, dedication, and accountability. We must embark on the green transition to stay competitive and comply with customer demands on the global stage. We find best practice solutions to standardize how the industry measures, reports and communicates about the green transition.

02.

Target group

As a member you’ll gain access to an exclusive network that promotes knowledge sharing, and that keeps you in the know on all the latest trends within the industry. You’ll be amongst some of the top OEM suppliers, who will be available to you for mutually beneficial sparring.

03.

Benefits

The network is especially geared towards top and middle managers, who wishes to gain knowledge about the most important matters related to sustainability in the wind power industry. All members work together constructively to advance sustainability for the benefit of everyone.

Topics

We define and pursue common industry practices, working in mutual accountability towards decreasing cost and increasing adoption of sustainable business practices. The network is by members for members. We encourage all our network partners to bring forth new topics and to take an active part in setting the agenda.

  • Current state analysis
  • Climate accounting
  • Product impact assessment
  • Establishment of ambassador corps
  • Internal sustainability education
  • Sustainability target setting
  • Communication plan
  • Network establishment
