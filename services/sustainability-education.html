Enroll in our academy and increase your level of environmental knowledge. We offer a wide range of educational services such as e-learning courses, workshop, coaching and much more. That way you and your organization can get up to speed on the things that matter.

Sustainability certificate

The Sustainability Management Certificate Education consists of six modules that provide you with the knowledge and competencies to safely navigate your company through the Green Transition and on to a sustainable future. Upon graduation, all participants who have successfully completed the courses will receive a certification as a “SustainX Sustainability Manager”. This certification stands for extensive gained knowledge and access to a great sustainability toolbox. The certificate is valuable as it proves having undergone hands-on projects that have been supervised by SustainX, which enables course graduates to drive sustainable change. You will be able to perform assessments of your company’s environmental impact and provide and drive optimal solutions to the challenges posed by the process of sustainable development. As you get equipped with the tools to tackle the natural obstacles within sustainable growth, you become an essential strategic asset as your company’s in-house sustainability manager.

01.

Intro & Sustainability

A module designed for all backgrounds that sets the foundation for your company’s work with sustainable development. Fundamental concepts and analytical tools are introduced and trained, qualifying you to asses your organization through a sustainability lens.

02.

Climate Accounting

Understanding the concepts behind climate accounting is inevitable in order to begin the work of reducing your climate footprint. This course is an immersive bootcamp training on carbon emissions and their ubiquity throughout your company.

03.

Sustainability Management

This module provides a stable structure for your sustainability strategy and trains your ability to set SMART goals. You learn to identify and relate to a baseline year, understand and define boundaries and visualize your company’s sustainability goals.

04.

Change Management

People may be reluctant to change, yet sustainable development is an aspirational, sought-after and welcome development. Learn how to inspire and enable your colleagues to navigate and apply the concepts of ‘responsibility’ in your green transition.

05.

Communication Strategy

Sustainability provides an important competitive advantage. Therefore a fine-tuned communication strategy can work wonders on your company’s branding. This module enables you to correctly communicate your sustainability goals and achievements.

06.

Circular Business Models

Succeeding with sustainable development relies on changing production and consumption patterns to circularity. In this module we take a deep dive into the circular economy and the circular business models. Get familiar with EU circularity models and much more.

Digital Education Platform

Take matters into your own hands and accelerate your sustainability journey. We have developed an extensive library of e-learning courses that covers the 17 SDGS, Climate Accounting, Circular Economy and much more. Complete the courses anywhere and at any time with our app-based learning tool.

Featured learning paths

01.

The 17 SDG’s

Is your organization working the 17 Sustainable Development Goals? This learning path contains 18 courses on the subject. Dive in to get a deep understanding of the individual goal’s objective, it’s targets and learn how you can utilize them in your organization.

02.

Circular Economy

Is your company working towards a circular transition, leaving behind the take-make-waste mindset? This learning path contains 2 courses that explore Circular Economy. You’ll get the tools to ensure a proper foundation for your organization’s sustainable transition.

03.

Climate Accounting

Is your organization in need of training on climate accounting? Dive into one of our 5 in-depth courses on climate accounting, so you can learn how to get an overview of your company’s sources of emissions and how to implement initiatives to reduce your organization’s impact on the climate

Workshops, conferences and leadership training

Workshops & conferences

Sometimes we need to see things in a new perspective to ignite our inspiration and inner motivation. SustainX’s mission is to increase sustainability in all Danish SMEs and provide you with the necessary tools to drive change for a more sustainable future. That is why SustainX offer to facilitate traditional workshops and conference days with our sustainability experts to enhance sustainability knowledge in your organization. SustainX Academy also offers to facilitate practical workshops with hands on experience and roadmaps for driving change. The workshops and conference topics are tailored to your organization’s specific needs.

Leadership training

Integrating sustainability in the core of the business has become a ‘license to operate’. A successful green transition also calls for new competencies and development skills at the management table. Leading an organization towards a greener future can be impossible without the necessary sustainable mindset. Building on a long track record of coaching C-suites, SustainX Academy offers one-to-one training in navigating the sustainability environment in your industry. A handful of online modules are explicitly designed for C-suites, equipping you with the tools to set the right direction for driving sustainability initiatives in your organization.
