About.

Your business
is our future.

SustainX supports your green transition and implements in-depth sustainable solutions in your company, from the management table to the production line.

Founded in 2020, SustainX works with Danish SMEs who want to contribute to the green transition. Whether you are starting from scratch or have set your sustainability goals, SustainX is ready to help you with a successful transition.

Culture statement

Vision and mission

Vision.

We all have a responsibility to tackle climate change and reduce carbon emissions. At SustainX we want to provide you with the knowledge, tools, and guidance to create greatness with a minimal impact on the planet. We truly believe that sustainability is not just good for the planet; it is also good for business.

Mission.

With this responsibility comes a mission. SustainX’s mission is to help all Danish SMEs towards more sustainable production. Our aim is 1% improvement every day. The goal is to ensure our planet’s resources, but also Danish jobs and Danish competitiveness on the international stage.

The Triple Bottom Line

We incorporate The Triple Bottom Line (TTBL) in our work. TTBL is a transformation framework that helps us move toward a regenerative and more sustainabile future. We expand business success metrics to include contributions to environmental health, social well-being, and a just economy. These bottom line categories are refererred to as the three P’s: People, Planet and Profit.

People.

A healthy work-life balance is key. We are working towards implementing a 4-day work week. We encourage an honest and open work environment and work hard to ensure employee hapiness.

Planet.

We want to make a postive impact on the environment. In 2030, we want to be one of the primary drivers for reaching the 70% emission reduction by reducing Danish SME’s CO2 footprints.

Profit.

We want to be able to support more businesses, dive into new industries and increase competitiveness. Profit-wise, we want to be able to hire more employees and attract the best work-power in the industry.

People

Our people are our super power.
